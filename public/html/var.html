VarDrugPub :   Variant-Gene-Drug Relation Database

  VarDrugPub is a database that contains genomic variants and pharmacogenomic information. There are numerous research studies published in PubMed about the association/relationship between drug responses and somatic mutations. We use a deep learning-based text mining technique to identify mutation-gene-drug relations in all the PubMed articles. You can use VarDrugPub at http://vardrugpub.korea.ac.kr.
